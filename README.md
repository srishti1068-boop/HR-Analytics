# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution designed to analyze workforce data, monitor employee retention, and uncover the key factors contributing to employee attrition. By transforming raw HR datasets into meaningful insights, this dashboard empowers HR teams and business leaders to make data-driven decisions that improve employee satisfaction, retention, and overall organizational performance.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

### 📈 Microsoft Power BI

* Designed and developed interactive dashboards and visual reports.
* Built data models to support advanced HR analytics and KPI tracking.

### 🔄 Power Query (M)

* Performed ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) operations.
* Cleaned and transformed raw HR datasets.
* Removed duplicates and standardized data for analysis.

### 📐 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

* Created calculated columns and measures.
* Developed custom KPIs and business logic for advanced reporting.

### 📊 Microsoft Excel

* Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
* Generated pivot tables and preliminary insights.
* Prepared and validated data before importing into Power BI.

---

## 📂 Data Source

* HR Employee Records
* Company Workforce Data

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Employee attrition can significantly impact productivity, hiring costs, and organizational growth. The primary objective of this project is to identify the major drivers behind employee resignations by analyzing workforce demographics, job roles, salary patterns, tenure, and job satisfaction levels.

The dashboard converts complex HR data into actionable insights, helping management implement targeted retention strategies and improve organizational health.

---

Track key workforce metrics at a glance:

* 👥 Total Employees

Gain deeper insights into workforce composition:

* Analyze attrition across different age groups.

---

### 😊 Job Satisfaction Analysis

Understand employee engagement levels through:

* Job Role vs. Satisfaction Matrix
* Satisfaction score comparisons across departments
* Identification of roles with higher disengagement and turnover risk

---

### 💵 Salary & Tenure Analysis

Discover patterns influencing employee exits:

* Analyze employee tenure before resignation.
* Correlate attrition with salary brackets.
* Identify turnover trends among lower compensation groups.
* Uncover critical retention opportunities during early employment stages.

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 🚨 Early Employee Turnover

Analysis revealed that the highest attrition occurs within the **first year of employment**, with **59 employees leaving during this period**.

### 💰 Compensation Impact

Employees in lower salary brackets exhibited significantly higher attrition rates, indicating compensation as a major contributing factor.

### 🏢 Department-Level Analysis

Interactive department filters allow stakeholders to:

* Compare attrition across departments.
* Drill down into specific teams such as **Research & Development** and **Sales**.
* Identify localized workforce challenges.
